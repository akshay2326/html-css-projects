Make Roads Safer with Traffix

Join us in ensuring road safety by reporting traffic violations and earning rewards for your efforts.

Why Report Violations?

  • Safer Roads: Contribute to a safer driving environment for everyone.
  • Earn Rewards: Collect points that can be redeemed for great prizes.
  • Make an Impact: Help authorities track and address traffic violations effectively.

How to Report a Violation

Follow these simple steps to report a traffic violation and earn rewards:

  1. Observe the violation.
  2. Document it with pictures or videos.
  3. Submit your report on the website.
