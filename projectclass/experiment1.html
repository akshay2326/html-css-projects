Traffic Rules

The following traffic violations are eligible for the 10% reward scheme:

  • Jumping a red light.
  • Over-speeding or reckless driving.
  • Driving without a helmet (for two-wheelers).
  • Using a mobile phone while driving.
  • Driving in a no-entry zone.

Reward Scheme

When you report a valid traffic violation with video evidence, you are eligible for a reward:

  • 10% of the fine amount is credited to your account after the fine is collected from the violator.
  • Rewards can be redeemed via online transfer or vouchers.
  • Keep track of your submissions and rewards through your account.
